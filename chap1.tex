This chapter serves to bring everybody to the same starting point, by quickly recalling the most important features of the underlying theory and the required practical tools.
We refrain here from giving a detailed introduction, which instead can be found in any standard textbook~\cite{Leader:2011gpt,Leader:1996hm,Peskin:1995ev,Halzen:1984mc,Kronfeld:2010bx}, and focus instead on practical problems, which we then discuss in detail in \cref{chap:rl}.
